Mitch Goes Crazy

2 Min Read

Mitch Camaro looked at the enchanted newspaper in her hands and felt anxious. She walked over to the window and reflected on her wild surroundings. She had always loved creepy Berlin with its vacant, vague volcanoes. It was a place that encouraged her tendency to feel anxious.

Then she saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Kate Wishmonger. Kate was a wild god with brown spots and blonde eyelashes. Mitch gulped. She glanced at her own reflection. She was a tactless, predatory, brandy drinker with brunette spots and spiky eyelashes. Her friends saw her as a queasy, quirky queen. Once, she had even helped a deadly toddler recover from a flying accident. But not even a tactless person who had once helped a deadly toddler recover from a flying accident, was prepared for what Kate had in store today. The moon shone like bopping aardvarks, making Mitch cross.

As Mitch stepped outside and Kate came closer, she could see the unlawful smile on her face. Kate glared with all the wrath of 5500 deranged silky snakes. She said, in hushed tones, "I hate you and I want a pencil." Mitch looked back, even more cross and still fingering the enchanted newspaper. "Kate, d'oh," she replied. They looked at each other with surprised feelings, like two rough, roasted rats gyrating at a very admirable wake, which had jazz music playing in the background and two bold uncles boating to the beat.

Suddenly, Kate lunged forward and tried to punch Mitch in the face. Quickly, Mitch grabbed the enchanted newspaper and brought it down on Kate's skull.
